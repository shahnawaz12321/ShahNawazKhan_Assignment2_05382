Eggity McEgg went for a morning stroll one day but what greeted him during his time
in the great outdoors was less than pleasant. BIRDS! SNAKES! LIZARDS! Everything's out to get him!
He must survive till Ms. Eggity Yolk comes with a flamethrower to burn all them critters to
kingdom come! Survive till the timer runs out and you'll be rewarded. Fail and you shall die a slow, painful death.
Use the arrow keys to navigate. You can use the up key to jump and the rest of the keys to move in that direction.
Pro tip: You can quickly tap the up arrow key to "climb" the screen.
